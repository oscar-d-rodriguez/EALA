Case Study

Component & Site Factories Achmea

One of the largest suppliers of insurance services within Netherlands and Europe

Business Situation

Key challenge was to reduce time to market. Stop reinventing the wheel with every new branded public site is created.

Solution

SharePoint chosen as the common portal platform across multiple brands. Focus on development of generic ("Lego" like) components leveraging the SCRUM Delivery model, followed by rapid SharePoint site creation based on such reusable assets

Milestone 1

May, 2012

SharePoint Component Factory established in May 2012 as a joined setup between the Avanade NL onsite team and the Bratislava DC near-shore team

Milestone 2

July, 2012

Component Factory fully operational, switching from the one week sprints into two weeks sprints. 7 FTE SCRUM team in Bratislava DC

Milestone 3

Octuber, 2012

Component Factory has been split into Site Factory and Component Factory. Running with the asynchronous 2 weekly sprint mode.

Milestone 4

February 2013

Contract has been prolonged till February 2014. After 10 months Avanade has delivered 42 components and 8 sites.
