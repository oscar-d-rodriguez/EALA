Case Study

Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW) Salt River Project

SRP EDW Mission provides accurate information to facilitate performance management and decision making in order to derive business value.

Business Situation

SRP is the nation's third-largest public power utility and one of Arizona's largest water suppliers.

Solution

SRP is structured as two entities: the Salt River Project Agricultural Improvement and Power District, a political subdivision of the state of Arizona; and the Salt River Valley Water Users' Association, a private corporation.

Milestone 1

December 10, 2012

Incorporation of the team into the project. No requirements were in place at the time, but a project deadline was established.

Milestone 2

April 1, 2013

UAT begins, and as the testing progresses, new requirements are being added to the project. The project’s Go Live date is not modified.

Milestone 3

May 15, 2013

Go-Live date. The first part of the solution is deployed successfully with a technical and business validation performed a priori.

Milestone 4

May 28, 2013

The second part of the solution is deployed successfully 2 days ahead of schedule. Business begins utilizing the solution in full.
