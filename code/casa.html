Case Study

AXA Group Life AXA France

Axa is the French leader for Supplementary Collective Pensions (1 billion € turnover, and 20 billions € of assets). It is present in 57 countries with 160, 000 employees and serves 102 million clients

Business Situation

In order to modernize its platform , AXA France has to replace its two Group Life legacy systems (MACAO, Axiome) based on Pacbase which will not be supported by IBM after 2015.

Solution

Accenture Insurance Services is providing BPO Insurance Services using TAIGA for collective products (ASP.Net based) TAIGA is managing the same kind of products for BNPP: 261000 affiliates the solution is to enhance TAIGA in order to support AXA requirements, migrate AXA data and provide hosting and AO support to their users.

Milestone 1

November 2012

Defense Portfolio: Implementation of a minimum number of upgrades necessary to enable the transfer. Starting recovery of Axiom contracts for defense Portfolio and Business Development

Milestone 2

November 2013

Web sites: Implementation of transactional web for web members and web enterprise. Implementation of new web site AXA provider. Implementation of interfaces and target processes

Milestone 3

January 2015

AXIOM Migration: Implementation of AXIOME product functionalities. Migration of AXIOME contracts in TAIGA. Implementation of New Features

Milestone 4

TBD

MACAO Migration: Implementation of MACAO product functionalities. Migration of MACAO contracts in TAIGA
